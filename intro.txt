------

ものか゛たりのはし゛まり

The Story Begins

It is 1997, the year
of the ox. Daichi-kun
lives in the quiet and
rustic Peach Land with
his friends.

のしまにしようではありませんか。」といって

One day, a lady named
Jaren appeared. "Why
not make a volcano on
the island
[and make it into a warm
and green paradise]?" she said.

(And so, she began to create the volcano.)
So she went to work...

 モーモーランドは あたたかく、みどりも

The island became warm
and verdant. "Jaren's
such a wonderful lady"
thought Daichi-Kun.

But since he had a wife
named Suzuran, he left
Jaren alone for the time
being...

 しかし、やーレンにはかこうからちていへと

But Jaren had a secret
plot to use a "Black
Path" deep inside the
crater to lure the Plutons
to the surface.

She could then rule
over the entire world.

れ、モーモーランドはかざんばいで おおわれ
はじめていました。

By the time Daichi-kun and the
others realized her intentions,
it was too late.

The Plutons had appeared, and
the island was engulfed in ash.
